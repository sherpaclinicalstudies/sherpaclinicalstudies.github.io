Frequently asked questions

What makes Sherpa Clinical unique?

Most sponsors continue to rely on traditional mass advertising - print, radio, etc. Yet these methods are wildly inefficient and have limited reach. At Sherpa Clinical, we use optimized digital marketing to target the right patients. We track our efforts in order to keep you informed, maximize yield, and minimize cost.

Why recruit online when our process has worked in the past?

Patients are increasingly empowered and searching for solutions to health problems online. 59% of patients go to the Internet to research a health concern before talking to another person. 72% of patients are willing to be contacted to participate in clinical research. 81% of patients are willing to provide health information to participate in research.

What's your team's expertise?

We have extensive experience and a deep network in patient recruitment, web development, and digital marketing. We've helped build startups and advised some of the biggest technology companies in the world. We love design and technology and enjoy working with customers to successfully recruit for clinical studies.

What about HIPAA, Security and Insurance?

We take privacy very seriously. All information shared by patients will be transmitted and stored in a secure, HIPAA-compliant way. We require sponsors to have clinical trial insurance which will cover any liability related to the in-person pre-screening and the trial itself.
