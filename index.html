Sherpa Clinical: Fixing clinical trial enrollment

80%

Of trial sites fail to meet patient enrollment

SOLVING YOUR UNDER ENROLLMENT

Failure to enroll necessary number of patients at each investigator site in a timely manner can compromise a trial’s statistical power and scientific validity. Trial delays prolong the time it takes to make new therapies available to patients in need.

$40kper day

Delays are a very costly part of clinical research

IMPROVING YOUR FINANCIALS

Delays also add to life sciences companies’ real and opportunity costs. Each day of delay can translate into ~$40K in real cost and ~$2.5M in opportunity cost.
