Frequently asked questions

What makes Sherpa Clinical unique?

Most sponsors continue to rely on traditional mass advertising—billboards, magazines, and radio advertisments. Yet the costs and effectiveness are sub-par. Unfortunately 70% of the possible patient population never hears about the trials which reveals how ineffective traditional advertisting is.

At Sherpa Clinical, we use modern marketing strategies to not only specifically target your ideal patient group, but also to use real-time metrics and continually update how and where to target your budget.

Why go online, our process has worked in the past?

Patients increasingly are empowered and searching online. 59% of patients go to the Internet to research a health concern before talking to another person. 72% of patients are willing to be contacted to participate in clinical research. 81% of patients are willing to provide health information to participate in observational research.

Beyond the current stats, all of the high quality marketing channels are moving online. The growth in the amount of time people spend online is rising at a rapid pace, which makes this an ideal time to start marketing online.

What's your team's expertise?

We're made up of digital marketing experts who've helped grow and advise some of the biggest technology companies in the country. Beyond marketing we understand business, design and technology to build some amazing tools for you to push your referral campaigns further.

What about HIPAA, Security and Insurance?

We take privacy very seriously. All information shared by patients will be transmitted and stored in a secure, HIPAA-compliant way. We require sponsors to have clinical trial insurance which will cover any liability related to the in-person pre-screening and the trial itself.
